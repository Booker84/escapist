THE ESCAPIST

Steve and Tom welcome you to The Escapist, Chichester's finest independant craft beer pub.
Our aim is to bring the best and most diverse range of craft ales and lagers along with wines and spirits to you so you can enjoy them as much as we do.
Based in the center of the beautiful historic city of Chichester we have a prime location for you to come relax and enjoy a drink with friends.
To go with your choice of refreshment we also have a large and eclectic range of music which is free for you to choose and ask us to play.
So please come and pay us a visit, we cant wait to see you.
